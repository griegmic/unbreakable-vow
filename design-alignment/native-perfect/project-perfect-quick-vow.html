Mobile-web parity: Preserves the current web structure: topbar, “Seal a vow,” vow card with inline verdict, horizontal suggestion chips, display stake, four stake choices, witness/share row, if-broken receipt, single primary continue CTA, guided fallback.

Native delta: “Add a witness” opens contacts/recent witnesses instead of starting with a generic share-link sheet. This is a high-confidence app-only improvement because the native phone has the address book and the social step is the product’s growth engine.

What not to add: No separate last-look screen. No extra education. No big dashboard chrome. Quick Vow is for returning users who already understand the product.

Remaining risk: The page is dense by design. If it feels crowded on smaller iPhones, reduce headline height before reducing vow input, stake, witness, or CTA clarity.
