Deviation Notes

Native Advantage: Apple Pay is primary because native iOS can make saved payment setup faster and calmer than a generic web Payment Element.

Native Advantage: The sealed screen opens Messages explicitly. The app does not imply it can text Joe silently.

Upgrade Candidate: The vow detail is action-led around “Text Joe,” because pending-witness is not an archive state; it is an unfinished social conversion moment.

No Witness Alternate: If the maker did not pick a contact, the invite screen says “Share the invite” and uses the native share sheet. The app should not invent a person or imply it can text someone silently.

After Messages Return: If the maker already tapped the Messages handoff, the detail softens to “Waiting on Joe” with “Done” primary and “Text again” secondary, because we cannot know whether the text was actually sent.
