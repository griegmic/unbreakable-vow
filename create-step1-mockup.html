What changed

  • Title → Prompt: "New Vow" replaced with "I vow to..." — the label IS the ceremony. Same font as input, flows as one statement.
  • Card wrapper → Free text: Input freed from the card. Larger serif font (26px). Reads like a declaration, not a form field.
  • The void → Smart defaults: Below the input, returning users see their last settings (deadline, witness, stake) as subtle pills. Fills the space with purpose. Communicates "we remember you" and "this is going somewhere real."
  • "Next" → "Set the terms →": CTA tells you exactly what Step 2 is. Gold gradient when active. Muted when empty.
  • Back → Dashboard: Explicit back navigation instead of orphaned page.

The Rork lesson

Rork's homepage works because the headline gives the empty space purpose. "Build real mobile apps, fast." fills the viewport with intention. The same principle applies here: "I vow to..." in large serif text gives the page a gravitational center. The space around it becomes contemplative, not empty. The input flows from the prompt naturally — type and you're writing a vow, not filling out a form.
