Smart Stack (recommended). All cards. Sorted: urgent (amber glow) → active (gold border) → witnessing (blue) → drafts (dashed) → completed (faded). No sections. Visual hierarchy does the work. Scales from 2 to 20 vows. No mode-switch from 1-vow hero.

Shreyas's Final Call
1 vow = hero view (unanimous). The vow is the page.

2+ vows = Smart Stack. The Hero+Stack creates a jarring mode-switch when going from 1→2 vows — the entire page layout changes. Smart Stack is the same visual language at any count. The urgent card's amber glow gives it the weight it needs without a separate layout mode.

The transition: When a one-vow user makes their second vow, the hero gracefully becomes the top card in the stack. No layout shock.
