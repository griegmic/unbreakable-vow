VERDICT
Proposed wins on 6 of 9 dimensions. The two areas where Current is equal or better (emotional timing, skip risk) are addressable:
  • Emotional timing — Proposed is only one screen later. The emotional peak doesn't evaporate between /witness and /seal (they're seconds apart). Wash.
  • Skip risk — Mitigated by visual hierarchy (share CTA is gold primary, seal is muted). System SMS catches phone-number witnesses regardless. Acceptable.
  • Dead links — Current's biggest problem. A witness who bounces from a spinner will likely never return. This alone justifies the change. Decisive.
